Contribution Guidelines

✅ Do’s

  • Original Content → Share prompts/templates that you wrote or customized yourself.
  • Clarity → Write in a clear, easy-to-understand format.
  • Relevance → Only submit prompts related to AI use cases.
  • Formatting → Use a consistent style.
  • Respectful Language → Keep prompts professional, safe, and inclusive.
  • Tested Prompts → Contribute prompts you’ve actually tried.
  • Helpful Notes → Add small tips for better usage.

❌ Don’ts

  • Plagiarism → Don’t copy-paste prompts from other sites.
  • Spam → Avoid irrelevant or self-promotional content.
  • Offensive Content → No harmful, hateful, NSFW, or unsafe prompts.
  • Overly Vague Prompts → Avoid unclear or generic submissions.
  • Misleading Templates → Don’t submit broken or non-functional structures.
  • Excessive Length → Avoid confusing, overly long prompts.

“By contributing, you agree that your submission may be reviewed, edited, or removed if it doesn’t meet these guidelines.”
